Frequently Asked Questions

How does image compression work? +

Our tool uses client-side compression algorithms to reduce image file size while maintaining visual quality. The process happens entirely in your browser, keeping your images private and secure.

What image formats are supported? +

We support JPEG, PNG, GIF, BMP, and WEBP formats for input. You can compress to JPEG or WEBP format for output.

Is there a file size limit? +

There's no strict limit, but very large images may take longer to process depending on your device's capabilities.

Are my images uploaded to a server? +

No, all processing happens locally in your browser. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
